# Privacy Policy for IGRemindMe

Last updated: March 2026

## What we collect
When you tag @remindme.insta in an Instagram comment, we collect:
- Your Instagram username
- The comment ID and post ID
- The time you requested for your reminder

## How we use it
We use this information solely to send you a reminder reply comment at the time you requested. We do not sell, share, or use your data for any other purpose.

## Data retention
Reminder data is deleted after the reminder has been sent.
